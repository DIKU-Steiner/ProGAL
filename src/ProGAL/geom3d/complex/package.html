A simplicial complex is a topological space of a particular kind, constructed by "gluing together" points, line segments, triangles, and their n-dimensional counterparts. [Wikipedia]

This package contains classes that are all related to three-dimensional simplicial complexes. The specific types of complexes (such as the Delaunay-complex) are implemented in their own sub-packages. All specific complexes use the CVertex, CEdge, CTriangle and CTetrahedron classes as the basic datastructure. It is possible to navigate entire components of a complex from any member of the complex. For instance a breadth-first-search from any edge can collect all tetrahedra in that component. The relevant methods for traversing a complex are:
